# IAG Track — Identity & Access Governance

Certifications for **WALLIX IAG**, the Identity & Access Governance product WALLIX
gained through its **May 2023 acquisition of Kleverware** (rebranded from "Kleverware
IAG"). IAG answers *"who has access to what, and should they?"* — access certification
campaigns, Segregation of Duties (SoD), and compliance reporting.

> ⚠️ Do **not** confuse IAG with inWebo. IAG ← **Kleverware** (acquisition). The WALLIX
> Authenticator MFA ← **inWebo** (technology partnership). They are unrelated.

tracks. See the
[product portfolio — IAG section](../00-overview/product-portfolio.md#5-wallix-iag--identity--access-governance)
for technical background.
